🔍 Spectrogram Analysis:

Spectrograms convert 1D time-domain signals into 2D images by showing how the frequency content changes over time. The x-axis represents time, the y-axis represents frequency, and the color intensity represents signal power. AM signals show constant frequency with varying amplitude, while FM signals show frequency variations around the carrier frequency.
